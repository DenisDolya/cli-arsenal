Terminal Browser (ncurses + libcurl)

---
Build
---
Dependencies:
  - gcc (C11+)
  - ncurses
  - libcurl

Build command:
  gcc -std=c11 terminal_browser.c -o browser -lcurl -lncurses

---
Usage
---
  ./browser <url|file>

Examples:
  ./browser https://example.com
  ./browser test.html

Controls:
  q – quit
  r – reload

---
Rendering Features
---
- Normal text: gray
- Headings (<h1>…<h6>): bright white
- <b>, <strong>: bold white
- <i>, <em>, <cite>, <dfn>, <address>: italic gray
- <u>, <ins>, <abbr>: underline
- <del>: strikethrough
- <mark>: gray text on yellow background
- <a href>: blue underlined, clickable
- <ul>/<ol> + <li>: lists with colored markers
- <blockquote>, <q>: rendered as indented quotes
- <hr>: horizontal ASCII line
- <pre>, <code>, <samp>: monospace blocks
- <img>, <video>, <audio>: shown as [link]
- <form>, <input>, <select>, <button>: ASCII form mockup

Block containers like <div>, <section>, <article>, <header>, <footer> are ignored (no extra borders).
